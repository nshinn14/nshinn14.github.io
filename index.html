Getting Started¶

You will first need to download the dataset from https://www.kaggle.com/START-UMD/gtd. This dataset contains information on terrorist attacks from around the world and it contains more than 170,000 cases. All credit for the dataset goes to the researchers at the National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism (START). More information on their organization can be found here: http://start.umd.edu/gtd/.

This tutorial will be working with a dataset in the form of a CSV (comma separated value) file.
